Mwai Kibaki

Biography

Mwai Kibaki (born 15 November 1931) is a Kenyan politician who was the third President of Kenya, serving from 2002 until 2013. Before entering politics, Kibaki served as Vice-President of Kenya from 1978 to 1988 and as Minister for Finance from 1969 to 1981. He ran unsuccessfully for President in 1992 and 1997, before winning the 2002 presidential election. He was re-elected in the 2007 presidential election.
